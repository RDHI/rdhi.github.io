Ray-Ding® Heavy Industries in Brief

Ray-Ding® Heavy Industries is one of the largest manufacturing companies acorss the galaxy and is the leading manufacturer of military/commercial spaceships and vehicles, defense and security systems, power plant reactors and systems, electronic devices and softwares, and service provider of aftermarket support. As Druili Federation's biggest manufacturing exporter, the company supports Druili Federation and other government/region customers in more than 2000 planets in 400 countries. RDHI products and tailored services include commercial and military starships and vehicles, spaceports, weapons, electronic and defense systems, power plants and reactors, advanced information and communication systems, and performance-based logistics and training.

RDHI, officiallyfounded in 2999 GSC, has a long company history. The company continues to expand its product line and services to meet emerging customer needs. Its broad range of capabilities includes creating new, more efficient members of its military and commercial starships family; designing, building and integrating military platforms and defense systems; creating advanced technology solutions; and arranging innovative financing and service options for customers.

With corporate offices in Planet Nanking, RDHI employs more than 1,715,000 people across the Druili Federation and in more than 500 planets in 124 countries across the galaxy. This represents one of the most diverse, talented and innovative workforces across the galaxy. Our enterprise also leverages the talents of millions more skilled people working for RDHI suppliers galacticwide.

RDHI is organized into four main business units: Starships and Vehicles; Defense & Security; reactors and power plants systems and electronics and softwares. which began operations September 9, 5015 GSC.  Supporting these units is RDHI Capital Corporation, a galactic provider of financing solutions.

In addition, functional organizations working across the company focus on engineering and program management; technology and development-program execution; advanced design and manufacturing systems; safety, finance, quality and productivity improvement and information technology.
